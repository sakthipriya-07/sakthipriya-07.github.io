10 Habits to boost your Self-Confidence and Keep your Mind & Body Healthy

Introduction

We all aware that Humans are creatures of habit. As per scientist's research, our brain will take 66 days exactly to change new behavior into an automatic habit. Even though we are realizing habits impacts our life either in a good or bad manner, we ignore paying attention to our everyday habits. Believe it or not, by doing simple changes in your everyday behavior you will see drastic improvements in your life..


Let's see the 10 habits that are going to change your life healthily.
  • Posture
  • Stop complaining
  • Learn new things
  • Spend quality time with your family & friends
  • Reading books
  • Exercise
  • Wake up early in the morning
  • Journal
  • Compliments
  • Keep your home & surrounding places clean
    •

1. Posture:

You may think this seems simple but trust me it will work. No matter whether you are alone or in public place try to pay attention to your sitting and standing position. Just sit straight and walk boldly, if you do this, with no time you can feel more confident and you will notice sudden changes in the way people are treating you.

2. Stop complaining:

Most of us gossiping and complaining about everything we have in our life without even in knowing. It's surprising, right? yes, it is. If you are a person who is saying I hate to gossip or complain about things and other people, try this - Take a piece of paper and write your thoughts about your friends, loved ones, and the things that you have. If all of your words and opinions were positive and pleasant, give some rewards to your self to appreciate. If not, from now onwards try to think positively

3. Learn new things:

By learning new things, you are building your brain muscle to be active and healthy. First fix a goal and give enough time for you to achieve that goal. For example, I am new to painting now, but within a year I'll learn and became a pro in painting.You can also fix monthly targets to track your progress. The more you learn, the more it will boost your self-confidence.
